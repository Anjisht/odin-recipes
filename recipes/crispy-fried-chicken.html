Crispy Fried Chicken

Description

This is the only crispy fried chicken recipe you'll ever need.
Why? It combines all the tried-and-true tricks to ensure the juiciest, most flavorful, crispiest chicken every time.

Ingredients

Chicken
Four pounds of chicken.
Buttermilk
Tenderizes the chicken and helps flour mixture to stick.
Flour
Gives a wonderful crust to chicken.
Seasonings
Paprika, Salt and Pepper.
Oil
Any vegetable oil will work.

Steps

  1. Make the Breading
  2. Dredge the Chicken
  3. Fry the Chicken
